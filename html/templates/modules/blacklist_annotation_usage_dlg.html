Blacklisting Annotations
Everybody has different interests - while some people like to annotate crowded urban scenes, others like to annotate still images. In order to let you focus on the annotation tasks you are interested in, there is the possibility to blacklist annotations.
After you've blacklisted an annotation task, the task won't be served again to you.
